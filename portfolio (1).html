Our Story

Pooja Mehta started Daily Loaf in 2020 when lockdown meant she had nothing but time, flour, and a sourdough starter she named Gerald.

What began as a passion project shared with neighbours quickly grew into Mumbai's most-loved neighbourhood bakery — now serving over 200 regulars every morning.
